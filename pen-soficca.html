The Problem

Access is only the beginning. Care is still fragmented.

The gap is not only access to treatment. It is what happens after treatment begins.

Medication access may be fragmented, but the bigger failure is continuity: follow-up, progress tracking, and next-step care are often manual, inconsistent, or missing.

Online access is starting to exist, but continuity still does not. That leaves room for a more structured treatment workflow.

Large underserved category
Hair loss is common, but structured continuity in treatment is still limited across LatAm.
No structured follow-up layer
Most current options help users begin treatment, but not carry case history forward.
